Have you ever encountered an alert like the picture above? If so, you could be at risk of breaches in you data privacy.

Definition: Data privacy is about keeping your personal information safe from other people on the internet, and you control over what you share and who sees it.

Why You Should Be Aware

Being aware empowers you to:
  • Make informed decisions about sharing your data
  • Demand stronger privacy protections
  • Recognize and avoid digital risks
Many people don't realize how much data is collected or how it can be used.
